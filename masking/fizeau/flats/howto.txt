For naco:
-Use conica_calibrate.script, put in the file locations etc at the top, and follow the instructions. Calls mdark2, lampflat2, twiflats2.
-If your final flat needs to be cut, or if you want to merge a twilight flat with a lamp flat, use merge_cut_flat_naco.script.

For nirc2:
-Use nirc2_flats to make flats. Put in options at the top and it does the rest for you. Calls mdark2.
-If you need to cut your flat, use cut_flat_nirc2.script.
