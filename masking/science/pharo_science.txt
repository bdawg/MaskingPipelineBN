Note: among these observations are things that are beyond what pharo can possibly do.
Using the available data from the Nidever paper: orbital period and mass of the primary,
we can estimate the semi major axis. With the parallaxes from Simbad, we can estimate the
angular separation. Things that are below 35 mas should not be observed ... here they come:

HIP 52940: sep ~ 21 mas
HD 174457: sep ~ 33 mas
HD 140913: sep ~ 11 mas
HD 30339: sep ~ 1.6 mas !!
