Description of idlvar keys from intermediate products in the idl masking pipeline

phs_v2corr - something that doesn't matter (blame Anthony)
v2 - vis2 
cp - closure phase
bs_var - bispectrum variance (errors^2) ??? - zero
v2_cov - vis2 covariance
ps - power spectrum (pixellated vis2)
v2_cor - vis2 correlation
cp_cov - closure phase covariance
avar - variance in v2 in a single frame of data due to atmospheric effects (typically small - extremely dodgy)
bs_v2_cov - covariance between bispectrum and vis2
bs_cov - bispectrum covariance matrix
cp_var - closure phase variance (errors^2) ??? - zero
err_avar - errors on avar
v2_all - vis2 from each frame
bs - bispectrum (mean over frames)
v2_sig - vis2 errors
cvis_all
bs_all - all bispectrum frames
cp_sig - errors on closure phases
mf_file - matched filter filename
u - coordinate
v - coordinate
